PKR™ Bot Commands

PKR™ is your Agile/Scrum “Planning Poker” bot for Microsoft Teams. Below are the main text commands you can type in a Teams chat, along with flags for customizing your rounds.

@PKR begin

Starts a new planning poker round in the current chat. By default, it prompts everyone to submit an estimate for 30 seconds on a “points” scale. You can override certain aspects with flags:

  • title "My Task" – (Optional) sets the round’s title.
  • time <seconds> – changes the round timer from the default 30 seconds to your chosen value.
  • participants <N> – if you want to override how many participants are expected in the round.
@PKR begin title "Revamp Website" time 60
Starts a new round with a 60-second timer and the title “Revamp Website.”

@PKR config

Opens a configuration card where you can set your default scale (Points, T-shirt, or Fibonacci), default round timer, enable/disable confidence input, and opt to have the bot send private (personal) config cards to your DMs or keep them in the current chat.

@PKR config
Displays your user settings card.

@PKR help

Shows a help card in the chat with quick actions and links to usage instructions. Use this if you forget the basic commands or want to explore more features.

@PKR help
Displays the PKR™ help card.

@PKR history

Lists some of your past estimation rounds, letting you review final results or re-estimate if needed. The following flags are supported:

  • limit <N> - Restricts how many rounds to show (default 10).
  • legacy - Includes older v1 data (from prior to May 2025) in the listing. Typically these are read from the legacy table, with numeric votes only.
@PKR history limit 20 legacy
Shows up to 20 of your old rounds, including any v1 (legacy) data from the legacy table.
