All-New PKRbot v2 Features

1) Fibonacci & T-Shirt Scales

Go beyond simple numeric estimates! PKRbot v2 supports both Fibonacci (1,2,3,5,8,13...) and T-Shirt sizes (S, M, L, XL, etc.). Select your preferred scale in the new round config card and let your team estimate in the way that feels most natural.

2) Anonymous Voting

Enable anonymous voting so your team members can estimate without feeling peer pressure. PKRbot v2 simply tallies the final numbers and reveals them without showing who voted which way—ensuring unbiased feedback.

3) Configurable Defaults

No more typing time 60 or anonymous true every single round! Now you can set default round settings—such as default scale, timer length, confidence scoring, etc.—in your personal config. The next time you type @PKR begin, those preferences apply automatically.

4) Connect to Jira

Link tickets to your estimation rounds and effortlessly send the final estimates right to Jira. Want your round’s mean + standard deviation posted as a comment in the actual story? No problem—just a single click from the results card!

5) New UI Cards

Configuring PKR has never been easier with the new UI-based configuration cards. Instead of typing every command, just fill in the fields for scale, Jira issues, anonymity, and more. The submission buttons do the rest behind the scenes.
